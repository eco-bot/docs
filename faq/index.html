    FAQ

    How do i Invite the bot

    1. Click on this link (This Requires Administor Perms for you to Invite the bot)
    2. Click on the server in which you want to add the bot and Click on continue.
    3. Click on Authorize and then complete the Captha.

    How do i know the bot prefix

    • The Default prefix of the bot is eb!
    • You can always mention the bot to know the prefix
    • You can also mention the bot to use it as an prefix

    How do I create Tags?

    Start off by usingeb!tag add <Name> <Response> simply because it guides you through the process and tries to tell you what went wrong if any. You can also use eb!help tag to know what else you can do with tags

    Why does the bot complain about requiring more permissions? I've given it manage roles already!

    The way discord decides if you can add a role or not is based on two things:

    1. Does the member adding the role have 'manage roles'
    2. Is the member trying to add the role higher in the role hierarchy than the role they're trying to add?

    Just assign the bot a role that is higher than highest role it has to assign, the role being hoisted does not have to have any special permissions.

    How do I get a message ID?

    Discord settings -> Appearance -> Developer mode

    How do I get the bot to announce new members joining?

    You can the eb!set welcome-channel <#channel> and the bot would post messages whenever a use joins

    Another bot has the same command and they both respond, what do I do?

    Currently, We dont have an command enable/disable system but you can change the prefix of the bot and use the new prefix

    What are Basic Permissions

    The bot basically requires the following permissions:

    - VIEW_CHANNELS
- SEND_MESSAGES
- EMBED_LINKS
- ATTACH_FILES
- ADD_REACTIONS
- USE_EXTERNAL_EMOJIS
- CONNECT
- SPEAK
